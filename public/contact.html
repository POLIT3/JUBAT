Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you deliver?

We offer same-day and next-day delivery options depending on your location in Nairobi. Contact us for exact delivery times.

What areas do you deliver to?

We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding areas. Please contact us to confirm delivery to your specific location.

Is the water safe for all ages?

Yes! JUBAT purified water is safe for infants, children, and adults. It's perfect for preparing baby formula and family meals.

Can I schedule regular deliveries?

Absolutely! We offer subscription plans for regular deliveries. Contact us to set up a customized delivery schedule.

How does the refill service work?

Bring your clean containers to our South B Branch and we'll fill them with fresh, purified water at a fraction of the cost of bottled water.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, M-Pesa, and bank transfers. Ask about corporate account options for bulk orders.
